Scoring Guidance

  • Severity (S): 1-10, highest is severe dam safety consequence.
  • Occurrence (O): 1-10, highest is likely/frequent occurrence.
  • Detection (D): 1-10, highest is difficult to detect early.

RPN = S x O x D. Residual RPN is re-scored after controls/actions.
